About Bridge

What is Bridge?

Bridge is a card game played in pairs. There are several stages to the game. The first is the ‘auction’ when the pairs try to work out how many ‘tricks’ they can win. After that the hand is played, and at the end a score is given that reflects how close they came to winnning the number of tricks they bid in the auction.

Find out more here

Why play It?

Bridge is a fascinating game that people never tire of. There are over 750 trillion possible combinations of bridge hands so it’s unlikely you’ll ever meet the same hand twice. Bridge has been shown to exercise your brain – stimulating both sides! It is a unique type of mental activity and a pastime that can last you a lifetime.

Find out more here

Is It difficult?

If you can count to 13 you can learn bridge. Learning in a fun and social environment makes it easier and having a friend or family member you can learn with is always helpful (though it is not essential to learn with a partner). However, Bridge cannot be absorbed, it has to be actively learned so the more time you can devote to it the easier you will find it.

Find out more here

Does It take long to learn?

In the past Bridge did take time to learn, however we begin with a simplified version of the game and it is possible to start playing hands of bridge after only one lesson! After that it depends how far you want to go! It takes at least two terms before you are ready to play in a club. After you can choose to learn as little or as much ommore as you want.

We keep Bridge FUN

Here at CBBridge we make learning Bridge easy. We believe in learning though doing and you will be able to start playing immediately. Our fully qualified teacher has a wealth of experience teaching at all levels and will steer you thorough your learning journey with understanding, support and good humour.

We provide lessons for all standards, whether you have never shuffled a pack of cards or if you are a master bridge player interested in the latest developments in bidding and play, we have a course to suit you. If you are not sure which class to sign up to just send us a message here.

Throughout the year we run a range of activities and events to engage and entertain you. Most of these are bridge related (games, quizzes and competitions), but we also have parties, graduation ceremonies and mystery tours!
